Goku's New Adult SSJ 4

Super Saiyan Adult Goku in *Dragon Ball: Sparking! ZERO* is a well-rounded and iconic fighter, representing Goku during the *Dragon Ball Super* era while in his Super Saiyan form. This version of Goku is known for his balanced stats and accessible playstyle, making him a solid mid-tier pick in most rosters. With 40,000 max health and a starting Ki of 20,000, he offers a decent foundation for players who enjoy a mix of offense and defense. His signature counter move, **Wild Sense**, adds a defensive layer to his kit by allowing him to evade and strike back at opponents, making him effective in tight exchanges.

Although this form doesn’t reach the overwhelming power levels of Goku’s later transformations like Super Saiyan Blue or Ultra Instinct, it still holds its own in battle thanks to reliable melee combos and ki-based attacks. He's especially suitable for players who prefer traditional Super Saiyan combat without the complexity of higher-tier transformation mechanics. However, against top-tier characters with god-level forms, he may fall short in raw power and advanced abilities. Despite that, Super Saiyan Adult Goku remains a fan-favorite due to his classic fighting style and nostalgic presence, serving as a strong, straightforward option for players who value fundamentals and solid character design over sheer power scaling.
